\textbf{Geometric Mean.}\\
If  $a_1,a_2,\ldots,a_n$ are real numbers, we define their \emph{geometric mean} as
$$G.M. =\sqrt[n]{a_1a_2\cdots a_n}$$
\bigskip

{\footnotesize
(We usually require the numbers to be non negative so the mean always exists.)
