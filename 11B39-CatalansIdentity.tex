Let $f_n$ be the \emph{n}th Fibonacci number $n>1$, then the following identity is called \emph{Catalan's identity}.
$$f_n^2-f_{n+r}f_{n-r}=(-1)^{n-r}f_r^2.$$
For $r=1$ we have Cassini's identity.
