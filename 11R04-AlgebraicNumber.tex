A number $\alpha \in \mathbb{C}$ is called an \emph{algebraic number} if there exists a polynomial $f(x) = a_n x^n + \cdots + a_0$ such that $a_0, \ldots , a_n$, not all zero, are in $\mathbb{Q}$ and $f(\alpha )=0$.
