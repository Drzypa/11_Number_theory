Let $p$ be a prime, $k$ and $n$ natural numbers.  Then

$$
  J_k(n) = n^k \prod_{p|n} ( 1-p^{-k})
$$

where the product is over divisors of $n$. 

This is a generalization of Euler's Totient Function.
