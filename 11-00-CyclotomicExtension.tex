\begin{defn}
Let $K$ be a field and let $\overline{K}$ be a fixed algebraic closure of $K$. A cyclotomic extension of $K$ is an extension field of the form $K(\zeta)$ where $\zeta\in \overline{K}$ is a root of unity.
\end{defn}
