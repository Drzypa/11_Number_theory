If $A$ and $B$ are non-empty subsets of $\integers_p$, then
\begin{equation*}
\abs{A+B}\geq \min(\abs{A}+\abs{B}-1, p),
\end{equation*}
where $A+B$ denotes the sumset of $A$ and $B$.
