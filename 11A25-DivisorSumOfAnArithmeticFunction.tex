Given an arithmetic function $f:\mathbb{Z}^+\rightarrow\mathbb{C}$, the \emph{divisor sum} $F$ of $f$ is defined by
\begin{equation}
F(n)=\sum\limits_{d\mid n}f(d)\text{,}
\end{equation}
where the summation runs over all positive divisors $d$ of $n$.
