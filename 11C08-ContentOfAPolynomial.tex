Let $P = a_0 + a_1x + \ldots + a_nx^n \in \mathbb{Z}[x]$ be a polynomial with integer coefficients. The content of $P$ is the greatest common divisor of the coefficients of $P$. 
\begin{displaymath}
c(P) = \operatorname{gcd}(a_0, a_1, \ldots, a_n)
\end{displaymath}
