For any positive integer $n$ and any real or complex number $x$,

\[ \sum_{k=0}^{n} (-1)^k {n \choose k}(x-k)^n = n! \]

Furthermore, if $n>m$ then

\[ \sum_{k=0}^{n} (-1)^k {n \choose k}(x-k)^m = 0 \]
