Let us begin with the product:

\[a_n = 1 + \prod_{i = 0}^{n - 1} a_i\]

Adding $1$ to $n$ and manipulating the result:
\begin{eqnarray*}
a_{n+1} &=& 1 + \prod_{i = 0}^n a_i \\
&=& 1 + a_n \prod_{i = 0}^{n - 1} a_i \\
&=& 1 + a_n (a_n - 1) = 1 + (a_n)^2 - a_n \\
\end{eqnarray*}
