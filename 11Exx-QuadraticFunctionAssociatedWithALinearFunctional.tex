Let $V$ be a real hilbert space (and thus an inner product space), and 
let $f$ be a continuous linear functional on $V$. Then $f$ has an associated quadratic function $\varphi:V\to\R$
given by
\[\varphi(v)=\frac{1}{2}\|v\|^2-f(v)\]
