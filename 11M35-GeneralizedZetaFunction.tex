The \emph{generalized zeta function} is defined for $\sigma \geqslant 1+\delta$
by
$$
\zeta(s,a) = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{1}{(a+n)^s},
$$
where $a$ is a constant. Clearly, $\zeta(s,1)= \zeta(s)$, where $\zeta(s)$ is the Riemann zeta function.
