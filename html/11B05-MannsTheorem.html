Mann’s theorem


Let A and B be subsets of ℤ. If 0∈A∩B,

σ⁢(A+B)≥min⁡(1,σ⁢A+σ⁢B),

where σ denotes Schnirelmann density.

This statement was known also as (α+β)-conjecture until H. B. Mann proved it in 1942.
