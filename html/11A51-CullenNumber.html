Cullen number


A Cullen number Cm is a number of the form 2m⁢m+1, where m is an integer. Almost all Cullen numbers are composite, and they have 2⁢m-1 as a factor if that number is a prime of the form 8⁢k-3 (with k a positive integer). The first few Cullen numbers are 1, 3, 9, 25, 65, 161, 385, 897, 2049, 4609 (listed in A002064 of Sloane’s OEIS). With the exception of 3, no Proth number is also a Cullen number.
