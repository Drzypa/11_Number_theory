table of small Knödel numbers Kn for 0<n<26


The following table lists small Knödel numbers Kn for 0<n<26. I’ve been deliberately arbitrary about what is small: for some n, especially even n, there are plenty of Knödel numbers to choose from so I only go up to 100; while for some odd bases there are so few I go up to 1000. And of course for n=1, the Kn are the Carmichael numbers, of which there is only one less than a thousand: 561.
