recurrence formula for Bernoulli numbers


This article establishes a well-known recurrence formula for the Bernoulli numbers.

The Bernoulli polynomials br⁢(x),r≥1 can be written explicitly as

br⁢(x)=∑k=1r(rk)⁢Br-k⁢xk+Br

(see this article (http://planetmath.org/CoefficientsOfBernoulliPolynomials)).

For r≥2, we have

0=∫01br-1(x)dx=1rbr(x)|01=1r(br(1)-br(0))

and thus

Br=br⁢(0)=br⁢(1)=∑k=1r(rk)⁢Br-k+Br

It follows that (still when r≥2)

∑k=1r(rk)⁢Br-k=0

so that

(r1)⁢Br-1=-∑k=2r(rk)⁢Br-k

Replacing r by r+1 and simplifying, we see that for r≥1,

Br=-1r+1⁢∑k=2r+1(r+1k)⁢Br+1-k=-1r+1⁢∑k=1r(r+1k+1)⁢Br-k
