Jacobi symbol


The Jacobi symbol is a generalization of the Legendre symbol to all odd positive integers.

Let n be an odd positive integer, with prime factorization p1e1⁢⋯⁢pkek. Let a≥0 be an integer. The Jacobi symbol (an) is defined to be

(an)=∏i=1k(api)ei

where (api) is the Legendre symbol of a and pi.

A further generalization of the Legendre symbol, due to Kronecker, is the Kronecker symbol.
