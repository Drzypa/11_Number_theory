corollary of Bézout’s lemma


Theorem.

If  gcd⁡(a,c)=1  and  c|ab,  then  c|b.

Proof.  Bézout’s lemma (http://planetmath.org/BezoutsLemma) gives the integers x and y such that  x⁢a+y⁢c=1.  This implies that  x⁢a⁢b+y⁢b⁢c=b,  and because here the both summands are divisible by c, so also the sum, i.e. b, is divisible by c .

Note.  A similar theorem holds in all Bézout domains (http://planetmath.org/BezoutDomain), also in Bézout rings.
