eliminated Sierpiński number candidates


Most numbers k are very easy to eliminate as Sierpiński number candidates, as it is very easy to come up with sequences of primes of the form k⁢2n+1. For example, for k=1 it is enough to mention the Fermat primes. For some of the seventeen Sierpiński number candidates when the Seventeen or Bust project began, only a single Proth prime, and it is often quite large. Eight candidates remain to be eliminated.
