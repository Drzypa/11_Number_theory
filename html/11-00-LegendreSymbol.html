Legendre Symbol.
Let p be an odd prime. The Legendre symbol (ap) or (a|p) is defined as:

(ap)={1if ⁢a⁢ is a quadratic residue (modp)-1if ⁢a⁢ is a quadratic nonresidue (modp)0if ⁢p⁢ divides ⁢a

The Legendre symbol can be computed by means of Euler’s criterion or Gauss’ lemma.

Generalizations of this symbol are the Jacobi Symbol and the Kronecker symbol.
