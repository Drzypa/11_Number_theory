convergence of Riemann zeta series


The series

∑n=1∞1ns (1)

converges absolutely for all s with real part greater than 1.

Proof. Let  s=σ+i⁢t  where  σ and t are real numbers and  σ>1.  Then

|1ns|=1|es⁢log⁡n|=1eσ⁢log⁡n=1nσ.

Since the series  ∑n=1∞1nσ converges, by the p-test (http://planetmath.org/PTest), for  σ>1, we conclude that the series (1) is absolutely convergent in the half-plane  σ>1.
