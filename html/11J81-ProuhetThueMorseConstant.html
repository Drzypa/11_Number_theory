Prouhet-Thue-Morse constant


The Prouhet-Thue-Morse constant is the number τ whose binary expansion is the Prouhet-Thue-Morse sequence. That is,

τ=∑i=0∞ti2i+1=0.412454033640⁢…

where ti is the Prouhet-Thue-Morse sequence

Another expression, not in of ti, is

τ=12-14⁢∏n=0∞(1-2-2i)

The number τ has been shown to be transcendental.
