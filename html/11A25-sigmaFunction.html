σ function


Given a positive integer n, the sum of the integers 0<d≤n such that d|n is the value of the sum of divisors function for n, often symbolized by a Greek lowercase σ. Thus,

σ⁢(n)=∑d|nd.

Sometimes this function is referred to as σ1⁢(n), highlighting its relation to the divisor function.

Given coprime integers m and n (that is, gcd⁡(m,n)=1) then σ⁢(m⁢n)=σ⁢(m)⁢σ⁢(n), meaning that the sum of divisors function is a multiplicative function.
