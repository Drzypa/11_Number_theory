plastic constant


Given the equation P3=P+1, solve for P. The only solution in real numbers is P=12+16⁢2333+12-16⁢2333=12⁢(9+69)3+12⁢(9-69)36≈1.3247179572447, and P is the plastic constant, also known as the silver number.

Another way to calculate the plastic constant is P⁢(n)P⁢(n-1), where P⁢(n) is the nt⁢h term of either the Padovan sequence or the Perrin sequence. For about n>20 the approximation is adequate for all practical purposes.
