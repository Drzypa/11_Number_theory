pointwise multiplication of a completely multiplicative function distibutes over convolution


Theorem.

Let f be a completely multiplicative function and g and h be arithmetic functions. Then f⁢(g*h)=(f⁢g)*(f⁢h).

Proof.

Let n be a positive integer. Then

(f⁢(g*h))⁢(n) =f⁢(n)⁢(g*h)⁢(n)
=f⁢(n)⁢∑d|ng⁢(d)⁢h⁢(nd)
=∑d|nf⁢(n)⁢g⁢(d)⁢h⁢(nd)
=∑d|nf⁢(d⋅nd)⁢g⁢(d)⁢h⁢(nd)
=∑d|nf⁢(d)⁢f⁢(nd)⁢g⁢(d)⁢h⁢(nd)
=∑d|n(f⁢g)⁢(d)⁢(f⁢h)⁢(nd)
=((f⁢g)*(f⁢h))⁢(n).

∎
