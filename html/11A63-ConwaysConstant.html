Conway’s constant


Conway’s constant λ≈1.303577296 gives the asymptotic rate of growth in the length between ai and ai+1 in most look and say sequences. That is, given a function d⁢(n) that gives us the number of digits of n in base 10, then

limi→∞⁡ai+1ai=λ.

For example, starting with n=1 and skipping ahead to a7, we observe
