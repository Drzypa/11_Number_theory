coefficients of Bernoulli polynomials


The coefficient of xk in br⁢(x) for k=1,2,…,r is (rk)⁢Br-k.

The proof is by induction on r. For r=1, note that b1⁢(x)=x-12, so that [x]⁢b1⁢(x)=1=(11)⁢B0.

Writing [xk]⁢f⁢(x) for the coefficient of xk in a polynomial f⁢(x), note that for k=1,2,…,r,

[xk]⁢br⁢(x)=1k⁢[xk-1]⁢br′⁢(x)=rk⁢[xk-1]⁢br-1⁢(x)

since br′⁢(x)=r⁢br-1⁢(x). By induction,

rk⁢[xk-1]⁢br-1⁢(x)=rk⁢(r-1k-1)⁢Br-k=(rk)⁢Br-k

Thus the Bernoulli polynomials can be written

br⁢(x)=∑k=1r(rk)⁢Br-k⁢xk+Br
