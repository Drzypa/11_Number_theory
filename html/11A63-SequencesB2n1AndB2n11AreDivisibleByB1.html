Consider the alternating geometric finite series

Sm+1⁢(μ)=∑i=0m(-1)i+μ⁢bi, (1)

where μ=1,2 and b≥2 an integer. Multiplying (1) by -b and subtracting from it

(b+1)⁢Sm+1⁢(μ)=∑i=0m(-1)i+μ⁢bi-∑i=0m(-1)i+1+μ⁢bi+1

and by elemental manipulations, we obtain

Sm+1⁢(μ)=(-1)μ⁢[1-(-1)m+1⁢bm+1]b+1=∑i=0m(-1)i+μ⁢bi. (2)

Let μ=1, m=2⁢n-1. Then

b2⁢n-1b+1=-∑i=02⁢n-1(-1)i⁢bi. (3)

Likewise, for μ=2, m=2⁢n-2

b2⁢n-1+1b+1=∑i=02⁢n-2(-1)i⁢bi, (4)

as desired.
