Demlo number


Given base b, a number of the form (bn-1b-1)2 for n>0 (that is, the square of a repunit) is a Demlo number, sometimes called a wonderful Demlo number.

The Demlo numbers for n<b are also palindromic numbers, and specifically of the form

n⁢bn-1+∑i=1n-1(i⁢b2⁢n-i+i⁢bi-1),

that is, the most significant digits are the first n digits of base b in order and the least significant digits are the first n digits of base b backwards.
