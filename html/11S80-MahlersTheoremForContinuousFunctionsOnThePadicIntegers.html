Mahler’s theorem for continuous functions on the p-adic integers


Theorem.

(Mahler) Let f be a continuous function on the p-adic integers taking values in some finite extension K of Qp, and for each n∈N, put an=∑i=0n(-1)n-i⁢(ni)⁢f⁢(i). Then an→0 as n→∞, the series ∑n=0∞an⁢(⋅n) converges uniformly to f on Zp, and ∥f∥∞=supn≥0⁡|an|p, where ∥⋅∥∞ denotes the sup norm.
