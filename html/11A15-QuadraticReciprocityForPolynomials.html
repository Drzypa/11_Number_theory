quadratic reciprocity for polynomials


Let F be a finite field of characteristic p, and let f and g be distinct monic irreducible (non-constant) polynomials in the polynomial ring F⁢[X]. Define the Legendre symbol (fg) by

(fg):={1 if f is a square in the quotient ring F⁢[X]/(g),-1 otherwise.

The quadratic reciprocity theorem for polynomials over a finite field states that

(fg)⁢(gf)=(-1)p-12⁢deg⁡(f)⁢deg⁡(g).
