Beatty sequence


The integer sequence

ℬ⁢(α,α′):=(⌊n-α′α⌋)n=1∞

is called the Beatty sequence with density α, slope 1α, offset α′, and y-intercept -α′α.

Sometimes a sequence of the above type is called a floor Beatty sequence, and denoted ℬ(f)⁢(α,α′), while an integer sequence

ℬ(c)⁢(α,α′):=(⌈n-α′α⌉)n=1∞

is called a ceiling Beatty sequence.
