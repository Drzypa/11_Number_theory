Let $P = a_0 + a_1x + \ldots + a_nx^n \in \mathbb{C}[x]$ be a polynomial with complex coefficients. The height of $P$ is
\begin{displaymath}
H(P) = \operatorname{max}\{\mid a_0 \mid ,\mid a_1 \mid , \ldots , \mid a_n \mid\}.
\end{displaymath}
