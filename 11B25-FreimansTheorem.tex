Let $A$ be a finite set of integers such that the $2$-fold sumset $2A$ is ``small'', i.e., $|2A|<c|A|$ for some constant $c$. There exists an $n$-\PMlinkname{dimensional arithmetic progression}{MulidimensionalArithmeticProgression} of length $c'|A|$ that contains $A$, and such that $c'$ and $n$ are functions of $c$ only.
